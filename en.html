Food, Breakfast & Lunch

Choose from a selection of breakfast and lunch options that will keep you full and satisfy your tastebuds. Drink a cup of coffee or wine alongside your meal for the complete experience!

Cocktails & Drinks

Once nighttime comes around, our bar springs to life, producing all sorts of delicious, refreshing cocktails for you and your friends! Choose the one that picks your interest from our menu, or request a custom drink that suits you perfectly.

Every so often we invite amazing artists to play music for a small audience live. Enjoy the tunes in a cozy environment surrounded by warm people.
